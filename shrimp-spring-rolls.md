# Shrimp Spring Rolls

## Shrimp

* Shrimp, halved
* Water
* Wine
* Bay Leaf
* Lemon Juice
* Salt
* Schechuan Peppercorn
* Star Anise

1. Add ingredients to pot of water and boil. Add shrimp for 1 minutes.

## Spring rolls

* Spring roll wrappers
* Basil
* Mint
* Cilantro
* Carrots
* Cucumber
* Leafy Greens
* Sprouts
* Rice noodles

1. Dip wrappers in warm water for 1-2 seconds.

## Peanut Sauce

* 1 tbsp peanut butter
* 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
* garlic chili sauce
* fish sauce
