# Bean Paste Noodles

* 2 oz white noodles
* 2 eggs
* 1/4 block tofu

## Sauce
* 1 tbsp bean paste
* 1 tbsp hoisin sauce (found in Asian markets; Koon Chun with the yellow and blue label is a good one)
* 1 tbsp soy sauce
* 1 tbsp broth
* 2 tbsp cup water
* 1 tbsp cornstarch, mixed with 1 tbsp water
* 1/8 tsp sesame oil
