# Bear Milk

* 3 ounces Jameson
* 2 cups milk
* 2 tablespoons honey or brown sugar
* 1 splash vanilla, preferably the good Mexican stuff
* 1 stick cinnamon
* 1 pinch nutmeg, plus a little extra
* 3 whole cloves

1. Combine spices with milk.
1. Bring to boil.
1. Strain and add to Jameson in cup.
1. Top with nutmeg.
